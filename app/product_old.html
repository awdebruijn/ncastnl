n-cast

Software

We support companies to create unique TV-channels at ‘out of home’ locations. We love what we do and we think it shows.

To date we have created thousands of channels to practically every corner of the world. Simple, safe, good looking & for every type of industry. We’ve been doing so since 1998.

Key features

Responsive Design, Browser Friendly, HTML5, Secure, Scalable, Skinnable, Multi-langual, Drag & Drop, Workflow management, E-mail notification
