n-cast

Software

We support companies to create unique TV-channels at ‘out of home’ locations. We love what we do and we think it shows.

To date we have created thousands of channels to practically every corner of the world. Simple, safe, good looking & for every type of industry. We’ve been doing so since 1998.

Key features

Browser friendly, Responsive design, Skinnable, Scalable, Multi-langual, Secure, Integrated workflow management, HTML5, Drag & drop, E-mail notification, Live feeds & social media integration, Template support, Integrated photo editor, Thumbnail generator.

New features 4.0.3

Looking Sharp

Our focus, when redesigning our User Interface, was to keep our Content Management System powerful yet fun & easy to use. We are proud to release n-cast 4.0 with a look & feel that appeals to all types of users, both technical and not. Including Drag-and-drop, generating thumbnails and letting you search, sort and tag all content. Built browser friendly with responsive design, n-cast will lead the way to the Future of Digital Signage.

Redefining borders

A new dimension in content Scheduling. N-cast introduces integrated GeoFence technology. Targeting location based content has never been so easy. Just select a position on the map and draw a radius. Every time the GPS of a player detects that it accesses a GeoFence, the targeted campaign will be shown on the screens instantly. In-vehicle Digital Signage, let’s move.

Adapting new Display Technology

N-cast has developed a SoC version of its player technology to support the new LG WebOS for Signage platform. The WebOS player is HTML5 based and supports different types of content including full-screen and multi-zone playback. WebOS for Signage eliminates the need for an external media player simplifying and reducing installation, operational and energy costs. Only requires a power and (wireless) LAN connection.

Content Management

Easily manage all your content by creating your own library. Upload media assets, video’s, images & logo’s from your desktop. Drag & Drop files on the fly.

Create new media by using powerful templates with eye-catching animations. Include live feeds (News, Wheater, Traffic) and Social Media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram), Full screen or as a ticker.

N-cast will generate thumbnails for you and let you search, sort and tag all content. Our integrated photo editor will help you crop, flip & adjust your images just the way you want.
